My studies

I obtained my Scientific Baccalaureate with honors in 2020. After that, I joined Polytech Clermont-Ferrand for an integrated preparatory class. After this preparation, I chose to specialize in the Maths and Data Sciences program, having already a passion for new technologies.

During my studies, I had the opportunity to explore various fields:

  • Deep Learning: I studied neural networks and their application in image recognition and natural language processing projects.

  • Machine Learning: I acquired skills in machine learning algorithms, data analysis, and predictive model creation.

  • Databases: I learned to design, manage, and query relational and non-relational databases, as well as optimize query performance.

  • Development: I strengthened my development skills using technologies such as C, C++, Java, Python, and SQL.

This versatile training allowed me to develop a solid foundation of knowledge and technical skills. It is thanks to this diversity of subjects that I was able to discover and cultivate my interest in development, both front-end and back-end. My academic journey provided me with a deep understanding of theoretical concepts and offered numerous opportunities to work on practical projects, thus preparing me to tackle real-world challenges in the field of technology.
